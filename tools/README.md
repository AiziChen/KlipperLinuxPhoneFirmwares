### Redmi NOTE4X KlipperScreen

This firmware combined with Ubuntu24.04 and Linux kernel 6.19.5 to perform best realtime ferformance and to reduce `Timer too close` on running Klipper for Redmi NOTE4x smart phone.


## How to use?
on Windows, open powershell, then type:
`
flash-all.bat
`
on macOS/Linux, open termnial, then type:
`
flash-all.sh
`
